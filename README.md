# Improvise & Open-Endedness

Research project exploring the intersection of improvisation and open-endedness — how improvisational practices relate to open-ended evolution, creativity, and emergent behavior in both human and artificial systems.

## Research Questions

- How does improvisation serve as a model for open-ended processes?
- What can open-endedness research learn from improvisational practices (music, theater, dance)?
- How do agents improvise in open-ended environments?
- What are the design implications for more-than-human improvisational systems?
