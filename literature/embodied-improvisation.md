# Embodied Improvisation

## Overview
Embodied improvisation centers on body-based, movement-oriented improvisational practices and their intersection with technology, somaesthetics, and interaction design. This domain bridges physical creative practice with computational systems.

## Somaesthetic Interaction Design

1. **Höök, K. (2018). *Designing with the Body: Somaesthetic Interaction Design*.** MIT Press.
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.7551/mitpress/11481.001.0001
   - Foundational text on somaesthetic interaction design. Proposes designing for first-person, felt bodily experience. Contact improvisation and movement practices inform design methodology. Directly relevant to embodied human-AI improvisation.

2. **Höök, K., Caramiaux, B., Erkut, C., Forlizzi, J., Hajinejad, N., et al. (2018). Embracing first-person perspectives in soma-based design.** *Informatics*, 5(1), 8.
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.3390/informatics5010008
   - Advocates for first-person design methods drawing on somatic practices. Movement improvisation as design tool.

3. **Höök, K., Eriksson, S., Juul Søndergaard, M.L., et al. (2021). Unpacking non-dualistic design: The soma design case.** *ACM Transactions on Computer-Human Interaction*, 28(6), Article 40.
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.1145/3462448
   - Explores soma design through practices including contact improvisation. Documents how improvisational movement reveals design possibilities that analytical methods miss.

## Dance & Technology

4. **Trajkova, M., Roemmich, K., Bala, S., McNamara, A., & Davis, N. (2024). Exploring collaborative movement improvisation towards the design of LuminAI — a co-creative AI dance partner.** In *Proc. CHI 2024*. ACM.
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.1145/3613904.3642677
   - Study of collaborative dance improvisation to inform AI partner design. Develops "Generative Model of Dance Inputs" — how movement choices emerge from interplay between self, partner, and environment. Key reference for embodied human-AI improvisation.

5. **Trajkova, M., et al. (2025). Bringing LuminAI to life: Studying dancers' perceptions of a co-creative AI in dance improvisation class.** In *Proc. Creativity & Cognition 2025*. ACM.
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.1145/3698061.3726925
   - Seven-month study of dancers working with LuminAI. Findings on how dancers perceive AI as catalyst, constraint, and co-creator.

6. **Trajkova, M., et al. (2025). LuminAI: Embodied AI as a catalyst, constraint, and co-creator in dance.** In *Proc. ICCC 2025*.
   URL: https://computationalcreativity.net/iccc25/wp-content/uploads/papers/iccc25-trajkova2025luminai.pdf
   - Analyzes how embodied AI transforms improvisational norms. AI can break habitual movement patterns and catalyze new discoveries.

7. **Alaoui, S.F., Bevilacqua, F., & Jacquemin, C. (2015). Interactive visuals as metaphors for dance movement qualities.** *ACM Transactions on Interactive Intelligent Systems*, 5(3), Article 13.
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.1145/2738221
   - Explores how interactive visuals can represent and respond to movement qualities in dance improvisation.

8. **Fdili Alaoui, S., Carlson, K., & Schiphorst, T. (2014). Choreography as mediated through compositional tools for movement: Constructing a historical perspective.** In *Proc. MOCO 2014*. ACM.
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.1145/2617995.2617996
   - Historical perspective on technology-mediated choreographic practice, including improvisation.

## Live Coding & Embodied Practice

9. **Bacot, B., Music, M., Music, M., & Music, M. (2022). CO/DA: Live-coding movement-sound interactions for dance improvisation.** In *Proc. CHI 2022*. ACM.
   DOI: https://doi.org/10.1145/3491102.3501916
   - Documents two-year practice of live coding with dance improvisation. Explores how technological constraints (live coding environment) and aesthetic constraints (mapping strategies) shape embodied creative practice.

10. **Hsueh, S., Alaoui, S.F., & Mackay, W. (2019). Understanding kinaesthetic creativity in dance.** In *Proc. CHI 2019*. ACM.
    DOI: https://doi.org/10.1145/3290605.3300741
    - Studies how dancers create through movement. Identifies kinesthetic creativity as distinct from visual or linguistic creativity.

## Contact Improvisation

11. **Novack, C.J. (1990). *Sharing the Dance: Contact Improvisation and American Culture*.** University of Wisconsin Press.
    URL: https://uwpress.wisc.edu/books/0265.htm
    - Ethnographic study of contact improvisation as improvisational practice. CI involves continuous real-time negotiation between bodies — a model for more-than-human improvisation.

12. **Kozel, S. (2007). *Closer: Performance, Technologies, Phenomenology*.** MIT Press.
    DOI: https://doi.org/10.7551/mitpress/7458.001.0001
    - Phenomenological analysis of body-technology interaction in dance. Explores how technology mediates embodied improvisation.

## Mixed Reality & Improvisation

13. **Lin, H., & Li, X. (2023). Here and now: Creating improvisational dance movements with a mixed reality mirror.** In *Proc. CHI 2023*. ACM.
    DOI: https://doi.org/10.1145/3544548.3580666
    - Explores how mixed reality tools can support dance improvisation by augmenting the body's self-perception.

14. **De Oliveira Barata, G., & Picard, R. (2017). Improvisation in dance with technological mediation: An investigation through studies of situation cognition.** *Cogent Arts & Humanities*, 4(1), 1367352.
    DOI: https://doi.org/10.1080/23311983.2017.1367352
    - Investigates how technology mediates situated cognition in dance improvisation.

## Key Themes for Open-Endedness Connection

- **First-person knowledge**: Embodied improvisation produces knowledge that cannot be fully captured in third-person description — challenging for computational modeling
- **Constraint as generative**: Physical constraints (body, space, partner) generate creative possibilities rather than limiting them
- **Somatic feedback loops**: Continuous body-environment coupling mirrors the feedback loops in OE systems
- **Technology as partner**: When technology responds to movement, it becomes an improvisation partner — a step toward more-than-human OE
